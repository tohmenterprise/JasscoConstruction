Commercial Scopes

Full-Service Interior Subcontracting

From framing to final finish, Jassco Construction handles the interior scopes that commercial projects depend on.

Metal Stud Framing

Load-bearing and non-load-bearing metal stud framing built to plan for offices, retail, and institutional spaces.

Drywall Installation & Finishing

Hanging, taping, texture, and finish work up to Level 4/5, ready for paint or wallcovering.

Acoustical Ceiling Systems (ACT)

Suspended grid layout and installation, acoustical tile, and specialty ceiling tile systems.

Specialty & Decorative Ceilings

Ceiling clouds, baffles, wood-look and specialty panel systems for lobbies and feature spaces.

Fire-Rated Wall & Ceiling Assemblies

UL-listed rated assemblies installed to spec to meet fire and life-safety code requirements.

Sound & Thermal Insulation

Batt insulation for sound control and energy performance, installed alongside framing and drywall.

Tenant Improvements & Buildouts

Coordinated interior scopes for new construction, renovations, and tenant finish-out projects.

Punch List & Close-Out

Detail-oriented final touch-ups and inspections to close out drywall and ceiling scopes cleanly.

Direct GC Coordination

We work hand-in-hand with general contractors and project managers to keep scopes on schedule.
