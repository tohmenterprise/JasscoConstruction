About Us

25+ Years of Commercial Drywall & Ceiling Expertise

Home / About

Built on Craftsmanship, Trusted by General Contractors

Jassco Construction has spent more than 25 years specializing in drywall and acoustical ceiling systems for commercial buildings. What started as a small crew has grown into a dependable subcontracting partner for general contractors and property owners who need framing, drywall, and ceiling work done right the first time.

We work almost exclusively on commercial projects — office buildouts, retail spaces, medical facilities, education buildings, and more — giving our crews deep familiarity with commercial-grade materials, fire-rated assemblies, and the tight schedules that come with occupied and fast-track jobs.

Based in the Bryan–College Station area, we take pride in being a local outfit with the experience and capacity to take on projects throughout the surrounding region.
